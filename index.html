Quick FAQ & Known Limits

  • Why no battery data? Some devices require manufacturer-specific protocols not exposed by standard BLE.
  • Can I request support? Yes. Submit a device adaptation request and attach logs/capture data.
  • Will this replace OEM software? In many cases yes, but a few devices still need vendor drivers for advanced controls.
