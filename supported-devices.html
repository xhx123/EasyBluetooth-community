Compatibility Matrix

Supported Devices & Protocol Layers

EasyBluetooth combines standard BLE telemetry with advanced vendor-specific integrations. Coverage continues to expand through community-driven adaptation requests.

Category A

Special Ecosystem Devices

Devices with special battery APIs or platform integrations, such as Xbox controllers，Playstation controllers and Apple AirPods families.

  • • Xbox controllers
  • • Playstation controllers
  • • AirPods / AirPods Pro / AirPods Max

Category B

Vendor 2.4G Protocol Unlock

Battery level support for 2.4G devices across different brands through protocol adaptation.

  • • Logitech / Razer / VGN / ATK
  • • HyperX / Rapoo / Mchose
  • • More brands added through community testing

Category C

Standard BLE Devices

Native support for compatible BLE keyboards, mice, gamepads, and audio devices that provide battery services.

  • • BLE keyboard and mouse devices
  • • BLE game controllers
  • • BLE headsets and earbuds

Need Support for Your Device?

Open a device adaptation issue and include your model name, connection method, and capture logs. Community contributors help validate protocol behavior and speed up support.

Reward note: when an unsupported protocol is successfully adapted and validated, eligible contributors can receive a 2.4G Protocol Unlock reward package (final approval follows maintainer review).
